Services

Swedish massage

This technique uses long strokes moving toward the body with a soothing oil to reduce stress and improve general health.

Deep Tissue

Deep tissue pinpoints specific areas of the body and helps them release with deeper pressure to penetrate the muscle.

Aromatherapy

Essential oils are added to massage oil and diffused in the room so maximum benefits can be reached. Customize the massage by discussing health needs with the therapist.

Hot Stones

Heated stones are placed on the body and used during the massage. The heat from the stones combined with the oil works to balance the body and re-energize the mind.

Acupressure

This personalized treatment uses the meridians of the body and circular strokes to assist in a variety of health concerns.

Headache Relief

This treatment is focused on the head, neck and shoulders using essential oils designed with the sole purpose of tension release during migraines and headache tension.

Lymphatic Drainage of the Breast Tissue

This light massage performed with corn starch helps in breast cancer prevention as well as assisting with post cancer complications.

Omega Massage

The technique uses an oil made with olives that have a higher concentration of Omega 3 acids, assisting with heart health and youthful appearance.

Cleansing Touch Wrap

A detoxifying gel is applied to the body and allowed to soak for 20 minutes before removal. A rich nourishing cream is added after the wrap for added softness.

Glowing Touch Scrub

Organic salt scrub is applied to the body, rinsed and followed by a nourishing cream for extra softness.

Epsom Salt Treatment

The salt treatment is applied to the body and allowed to set in to the muscles for 20 minutes, rinsed and followed by a nourishing cream for extra softness.
